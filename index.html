This web portfolio includes examples of my work in Mr. Lanus' Web Design Class. It also includes my reflection on what I learned in each unit of the course.

Unit 1: HTML

  1. What do you think are the most important vocabulary terms (or key concepts) in this unit? What do you think that they mean?
    • The most important vocabulary terms are paragraph, div, main, unordered list. They are all part of putting a great webpage together from scratch.
  2. What do you think are the most important skills that you learned in this unit of study?
    • The most important skills that I have learned in this unit is lists because they allow us to be able to display more information to the user.
  3. In what ways have you gotten better at this kind of work?
    • I have gotten better than I was before because now I understand the way to build my website out of noting, before I had no clue what to do.
  4. In what ways do you think you need to improve?
    • The way that I can improve is by being able to make my website have design to it so that it looks presentable and people are shocked on how good it looks
  5. What problems did you encounter while you were working on this piece? How did you solve them?
    • The problems that I encountered when I was working on this piece was I put a div where it wasn't suppose to be and it bolded my work
  6. What was especially satisfying to you about either the process or the finished product?
    • The thing that was most satisfying is that I was able to do something that I was not able to do anymore
  7. If you were the teacher, what comments would you make about this piece?
    • The comments that I would make is that it's getting there but you can still use some work to make it look like a godd well designed page
