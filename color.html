In this assignment I was asked to make color schemes that represnt the clients that were given to me, I had to choose what color scheme goes with the clients name.

Kindergarten Teacher

Expalnation

The reason why I chose these color schemes for the kindergarten teacher is because when I think of a kindergarten teacher I think of bright colors such as red, blue, green and etc.,. Usually kindergarten teachers represent fun and happiness, with all these colors this attracts the students to be more eager to learn knowing that it will be fun just by looking at those colors.

Landscape Architect

Explanation

I chose these color schemes for a landscape architect because when I think of the name Landscape Architect I think of a dark green color that can represent plants, grass and trees, just everything it has to do with landscape.
